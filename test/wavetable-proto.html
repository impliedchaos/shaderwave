Wavetable — all 64 baked frames

The waveform you see (and hear) is read back from a baked CPU table — 64 frames × 1024 samples, the single source of truth the real engine will share with the GPU texture, the oscillator scopes, and the LFO shapes. Morph crossfades the four keyframes Triangle → Sine → Square → Saw. Audio here is the naive full-bandwidth table (prototype only) — it'll alias up high until per-octave band-limited mips are added; that's why playback is pitched low.
